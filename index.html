Connect your Google Sheet

No accounts or logins needed here — just publish your sheet and paste the link. One-time setup, takes 2 minutes.

1
Open your sheet in Google Sheets

Go to sheets.google.com. If your data is still in Excel, go to File → Import to bring it in.

2
Publish the "DATA GEOFF" sheet

Click File → Share → Publish to web → change the first dropdown from "Entire Document" to "DATA GEOFF" → change the second dropdown to CSV → click Publish → copy the link.

3
Paste the link below

It looks like https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/...

Stored only in this browser. Your sheet stays in your Google account.
